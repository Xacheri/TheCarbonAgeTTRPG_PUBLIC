Carbodiesel is a powerful, mid-range in price, high-yield combustion fuel developed by the [[United States]] during [WWII](../Historical%20Ledger/WWII.md) for the dual purposes of crafting more powerful bombs and more potent fuel for tanks and aircraft.

Of the three primary Petrochems, carbodiesel exhaust has the highest GWP, and is considered the primary player in the world's climate apocalypse.

Another factor contributing to the unique impact of Carbodiesel is it's ability to carbonize and re-bond with the things it burns, creating more slightly-weaker fuel in a slowly shrinking loop.
