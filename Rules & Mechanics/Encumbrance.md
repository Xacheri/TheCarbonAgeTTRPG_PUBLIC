Standard encumbrance limits you to carrying 15 times your Strength score in pounds before your speed drops to 5 feet. You can push/drag/lift temporarily up to 30 times your Strength score in pounds. If your encumbrance exceeds the limit that you can push/drag/lift, your speed drops to 0 feet.

| **STR SCORE** | **Carry Weight** | Push/Pull Weight | **"Stopping" Weight** |
| ------------- | ---------------- | ---------------- | --------------------- |
| **8**         | 120              | 240              | >240                  |
| **9**         | 135              | 270              | >270                  |
| **10**        | 150              | 300              | >300                  |
| **11**        | 165              | 330              | >330                  |
| **12**        | 180              | 360              | >360                  |
| **13**        | 195              | 390              | >390                  |
| **14**        | 210              | 420              | >720                  |
