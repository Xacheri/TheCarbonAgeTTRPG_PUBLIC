For the purpose of this game, guns are not exotic weapons at all. 

**Handguns** - If you are proficient with any simple ranged weapon, then you are proficient with handguns.

**Rifles and Shotguns** - If you are proficient with any martial ranged weapon, then you are proficient with Rifles and Shotguns

**Explosives** - Explosives are equipment, shot from a rifle, or a thrown weapon. If thrown, you are proficient if you are proficient in any thrown weapon.
