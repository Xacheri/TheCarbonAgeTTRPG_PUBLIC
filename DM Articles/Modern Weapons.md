>While the specific firearm you equip may vary slightly from these base stat blocks (different ammo capacity / weight / range), you can assume it has the values from the category it fits into unless told otherwise.
>The regular versions of items still exist, particularly for Melee weaponry, though they are typically assembled from pieces of modern junk, unless you can find a rare Medieval blacksmith.

# Ranged
---
## Pistol, Automatic
- **Damage:** 2d6 piercing
- **Weight:** 3 lb.
- **Properties:** Ammunition (range 50/150), reload (15 shots)
- **Examples:** Glock 17, Beretta 92FS, SIG Sauer P320, Smith & Wesson M&P9 M2.0
## Revolver
- **Damage:** 2d8 piercing
- **Weight:** 3 lb.
- **Properties:** Ammunition (range 40/120), reload (6 shots)
- **Examples:** Colt Python, Smith & Wesson Model 686, Ruger GP100
## Rifle, Hunting
- **Damage:** 2d10 piercing
- **Weight:** 8 lb.
- **Properties:** Ammunition (range 80/240), reload (5 shots), two-handed
- **Examples:** Remington Model 700, Winchester Model 70, Ruger American Rifle
## Rifle, Automatic
- **Damage:** 2d8 piercing
- **Weight:** 8 lb.
- **Properties:** Ammunition (range 80/240), burst fire, reload (30 shots), two-handed
- **Examples:** Colt M4 Carbine, Ruger AR-556, Smith & Wesson M&P15, FN SCAR 16S
## Shotgun
- **Damage:** 2d10 piercing
- **Weight:** 7 lb.
- **Properties:** Ammunition (range 30/90), reload (2 shots), two-handed
- **Examples:** Remington 870, Mossberg 500, Benelli M4, Franchi Affinity 3

## Chainsaw
- **Damage:** 2d8 piercing
- **Weight**: 15 lb
- **Properties**: Heavy, Two-handed, Ammunition, Melee, Reload (1 pint of Petrochem / 12 minutes of use)
- **Special**: All attacks against inanimate objects are critical hits. On critical hits with this weapon, you can roll one of the weapon’s damage dice one additional time and add it to the extra damage of the critical hit.
