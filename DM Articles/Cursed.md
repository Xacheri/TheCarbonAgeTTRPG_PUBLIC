A rare **post-Calamity mutational deviation**: horn growth, tails, exotic skin color, heat-resistant dermis patches, and an innate sensitivity to the Arcane. They are almost exclusively descendants of individuals who contracted but did not succumb to the Cursed mutagen at the end of the **[Calamity (The Calamity)](Historical%20Ledger/Calamity%20(The%20Calamity).md)**. Some communities treat them as omens; other regard them with pity and disgust.
