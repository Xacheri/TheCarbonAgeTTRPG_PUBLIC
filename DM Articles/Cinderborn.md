 A stocky, hardy **subterranean-adapted mutant human** lineage descended from long-term bunker and mine populations. Dense bone, high pain tolerance, soot-stained skin that doesn’t sunburn so much as char, and lungs adapted to particulates. Renowned mechanics and fabricators of petro-chem vehicles and heavy equipment; many are brilliant builders but (by culture, not biology) often prefer wrenching to piloting. Can pass as human at a glance, but the build and skin give them away.
