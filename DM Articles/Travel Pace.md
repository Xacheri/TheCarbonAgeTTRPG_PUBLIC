A party can travel at speeds determined by the chart below. 

A handful of things can modify the pace a party travels at, including:
- **Difficult Terrain** - A party travels at half their Pace's speed through difficult terrain.
- **Difficult Terrain** - A party travels at half their Pace's speed at high altitudes, if unaccustomed.
- **Cold Weather** - A party travels at half their pace through cold environments that they are unprepared for.
- **Injury / Unconscious Party Member** - A party travels at half their pace to assist a party member who is unconscious or who moves slower than half the speed of the party due to an injury.
