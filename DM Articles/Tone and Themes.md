*The Carbon Age* is a post-apocalypse diesel-punk setting and the game played in this setting should follow a tone that aligns with that. 

This game will prioritize elements like **survival**, **scavenging**, and **tactical combat**. The survivors of this world are often ruthless and resourceful, but the communities that survive are ones built on trust and security.

Enemies in this game will be things like mutated creatures, raiders, enemy humans, and automata. Large diesel-punk vehicles will be an element of play, though most vehicles usefulness are hampered by a general lack of maintained roads. Guns are the primary weapons of the age, though melee weapons have their usefulness in the hands of skilled practitioners, especially considering the scarcity of ammo.

Some of the themes in this setting are dark, but nothing you wouldn't be able to air on basic cable TV channels. Violence, drugs, and alcohol are present in the setting but nothing overtly sexual is written in, because that's gross on a tabletop game.

When playing this game, have fun shooting your way through mutants and bringing down lighting strikes on raiders with the power of The Flux but be mindful of managing your ammunition, food, and water as you travel through the ruins of a gas-obsessed 1980s America.
