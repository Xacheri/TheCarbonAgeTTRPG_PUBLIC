# Carbon Age Player-Facing Wiki
Hello traveler! 

Welcome to the Carbon Age Wiki! This is a public repository of rules, lore, and wordbuilding for my Carbon Age TTRPG setting. If you came here looking for examples of my code, I am sorry to disappoint.

Well, since you've come all this way, let me tell you a little bit about my world...

## In A Nutshell
At it's core, *The Carbon Age* is an alternate-history post-apocalyptic setting with elements of sci fi, science fantasy, and dark satire.

In this setting, the world collapsed from a **climate-apocalypse** in the **1980s** driven by an **overconsumption** of new, dangerous **petrochems** which took humanity by storm far harder than the nuclear science discovered by the Manhattan Project in our modern world.

In *The Carbon Age*, violence, genetic mutations, and disease run rampant as humanity clings to it's last smog-tainted breath. The few remaining heroes in this world act as a glimmer of hope that civilization may rise from it's ashes, while the multitudinous scoundrels raise the question of whether it really deserves to.   

Campaigns in this setting are meant to have a darker, survival element to them and I encourage you to run them with rules around food, water, encumbrance, and exhaustion. I have some spreadsheets to help your players track those things, and automatically consume them when needed : - )
