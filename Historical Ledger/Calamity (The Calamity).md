The Calamity describes the series of geological, magnetic, and meteorological events that occurred in [[1984.md]] that lead to the fall of modern civilization and the beginning of the Carbon Age. 

While the conditions that could cause the Calamity were created by Man via climate change caused by rapid and ubiquitous adoption of Carbon Age petrochems, the event itself was triggered and accelerated by extradimensional intervention on behalf of [[The Others]].

The Calamity officially started in [July 1984](1984.md#July%201984), when the [[Atlantic Super-Typhoons]] moved inland from the coast. In the past, these typhoons lost steam over land, but resource reconnaissance from [The Others](The%20Others.md) had left anomalous energy fields that fed into the storms, increasing their intensity as they moved inland.

The rapid and unnatural pace at which these storms gained strength alarmed the [United States](United%20States.md) government. Ronald Reagan, who had at this point bordered on drug-induced mania, held a conviction that [China](China.md) had completed construction of a weather machine, and they were using it to assault the US.

In "retaliation" to these storms which had destroyed key civilian and military targets in the U.S., [Supermassive Yield Carbo-Diesel Bomb](Supermassive%20Yield%20Carbo-Diesel%20Bomb)s were launched towards China, painting the nation in black oil and fire. This would be the last great act from the government of the [United States](United%20States.md) as it would collapse in the coming months from resource scarcity and logistical challenges.

In the coming months, more freak "natural" incidents would contribute for dismantling other world governments. 

[The Others](The%20Others.md) would influence another event as they attempted to siphon a portion of magnetic energy from the earth, flipping the poles and frying electronics worldwide. This event would also trigger the first [Starfolk](Starfolk.md) mutations in Artic dwelling communities.

This failed extraction also temporarily super-heated the metal-rich lower crust underneath the poles, causing permafrost to thaw and sea levels to rise, burying islands under the sea and reviving diseases like the [[Black Death]]. 

Lacking the infrastructure to distribute vaccines to reliably ensure herd immunity, diseases killed off billions of people. Even today, Vaccines are a rare and coveted resource and certain organizations work to kill confirmed vaccinated folks to manufacture treatments from their blood.

The final disaster of the Calamity was the result of a mutagen that had begun to manifest in some humans. This mutation caused 80% of people to develop it to develop a host of reptilian features and defects before dying of organ failure, and occurred through a large portion of the population. The 20% that remain begin to carry the [Cursed](Cursed.md) gene, which causes their offspring to be born a [Cursed](Cursed.md) mutant.
